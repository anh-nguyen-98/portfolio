Who is Nam Anh?


Anh was born in Vietnam and currently lives in Saigon.

Anh is currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science at Fulbright University Vietnam and an incoming Software Engineer. Anh's logic-driven nature and insatiable appetite for solving puzzles are what fueled her love for coding.

Anh has experience in developing both front-end (HTML, CSS) and back-end (REST, SQL database) for stateless and dynamic site with state-of-the-art frameworks like Spring.

Anh has great attention to detail and problem solving and good time management and ability to work efficiently and effectively with people from all levels and backgrounds.
